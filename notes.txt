## How to convert README.md to README.pdf:
  ~/bin/iCloud/Markdown.pl README.md > README.html
  open README.html in Safari
  File->Print
    deselect "Print Headers and Footers"
    Select PDF
      navigate to the source to KioskContacts
      change file name to README.pdf
      save
